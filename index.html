Our Menu Book a Table

The Ming Room serves authentic Cantonese cuisine and offers continuous new creative variations.

The Ming Room opened in August 2003 and is reputed as the most established Chinese restaurant in The Bangsar & Damansara area. Recently refurbished, it re-opened in July 2009 to offer guests an event better experience.

  • Some of the new must-try specialties are Baked Foie Gras with stuffed Sea Clams and Steamed Fresh Water Prawn with Radish Cake.
  • The restaurant also serves an excellent range of Hong Kong Dim Sum for lunch.
  • Timeless favourites like the Double-Boiled Superior Shark’s Fin Soup with Wanton in Pumpkin, River Camp Medallion in superior stock and Chilled Durian Pudding remain popular with our patrons.

Be impressed with shinning renovation that blends history & elegance with comfort cutting edge interior interposed with terracotta horses creates the perfect venue to host any corporate or personal celebrations in the 300 pax capacity hall of the 6 private dining rooms.
